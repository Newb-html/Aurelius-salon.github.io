Hello, I'm Josh. I'm a Colorado native, and was raised in Fort Collins Colorado. After graduation I joined the United States Navy and served for 20+ years. After retiring as a Chief Petty Officer I moved back, and currently reside in the Denver metro area. With a passion for everything artistic, I decided to go into the beauty industry to both pursue a career in the arts, and make people feel beautiful while in the process. I had a drive and passion for owning my own business far before my retirement from the service. I attended Paul Mitchell of Lakewood, and excelled far beyond my peers as one of the top stylists in the school. I have attended multiple courses in my industry and have a certification in eyelashes, extensions, cutting, and am Master of Balayage graduate. At Aurelius Salon, I not only want you to love your hair, but have an incredible experience while doing so. You can expect the upmost respect and care not only as a person, but for your hair too. ​
